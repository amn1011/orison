How We Print

DTF (Direct to Film)

Yeh modern printing ka king hai. Isme hum advance machines se design ko ek special film par print karke, powder adhesive ki madad se directly fabric par high temperature par heat-press karte hain. Iski wash life behtareen hoti hai aur details sharp aati hain.

Sublimation Printing

Polyester fabric ke liye sabse best option! Isme ink gas form me convert hokar direct fabric ke particles se merge ho jati hai. Is technology se kiya gaya print kabhi fade nahi hota, na hi crack hota hai — bilkul lightweight feeling deta hai.

Premium Quality Tees

Hum sirf standard prints nahi karte, balki comfortable, breathable fabric select karte hain jo premium looks ke sath durable hon. Orison Teeworks ka focus quality aur custom orders par rehta hai.
