Shipping & Delivery

We deliver your creativity straight to your doorstep across India.

Order Processing Time

Since we custom-print every item, it takes 1-2 business days to craft your product perfectly. Quality takes time, but we promise it's worth the wait!

Estimated Delivery

Once dispatched, most orders reach you within 3 to 7 business days depending on your location. We ship via premium courier partners like Delhivery, BlueDart, and Ecom Express.

Shipping Charges

We believe in simple pricing. Flat shipping rate of ₹49 for all prepaid orders. Orders above ₹999 qualify for Free Shipping!

Tracking Your Order

As soon as your package leaves our facility, you will receive an AWB tracking link via WhatsApp/Email. You can also track it anytime on our Track Order page.

Important Note:

Delivery timelines may be slightly affected by public holidays, extreme weather conditions, or courier partner delays. If your order doesn't arrive within 10 days, please message us immediately and we will investigate it for you.
