Our Transparency Promise

No hidden rules. Just a fair, simple, and transparent commitment to your custom prints.

At Orison Teeworks, we value the trust you place in us to print your creativity. These terms are designed to ensure a seamless, high-quality, and completely fair experience for both of us.

1. 100% Satisfaction & Replacement Guarantee

Since every t-shirt is uniquely crafted just for you, we cannot accept general returns if you change your mind. However, your satisfaction is our top priority! If your package arrives damaged, has a stitching defect, or the print has a manufacturing error, just let us know within 48 hours of delivery. We will print a fresh new one and ship it to you free of cost—no questions asked!

2. What You See is (Almost Exactly) What You Get

We use industry-grade DTF (Direct to Film) and Sublimation techniques to make colors pop beautifully. Please note that mobile/computer screens display colors in RGB, while fabric printing uses CMYK inks. This might cause a very minor variation (around 5-10%) in brightness or exact shade. We work hard to make it look as close to your screen design as technically possible!

3. Finding the Perfect Fit

To avoid any fitting surprises, we display a detailed Size Chart next to every product page. We highly recommend measuring your favorite t-shirt before selecting a size. Because items are custom-printed on demand, we cannot exchange shirts due to incorrect size choices, but our support team is always here to guide you before you click buy!

4. Your Art, Your Responsibility

You retain full ownership and copyright of any image, text, or logo you upload for printing. By submitting a design, you guarantee that you have the right to use it. Orison Teeworks acts purely as your printing partner, meaning any legal responsibilities regarding custom images belong solely to the customer.

5. Easy Cancellation Window

Made a typo in your custom text or chose the wrong color by mistake? Don't worry! You can completely cancel or modify your order within 3 hours of placing it. Once it enters our custom printing queue after that window, the fabric cannot be unprinted, and production cannot be stopped.

Have a question before ordering?

We are just a message away! Connect with us directly to clarify sizing, designs, or bulk orders.
